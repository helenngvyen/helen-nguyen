Hi! I’m Helen, a multidisciplinary
designer living in Vancouver, Canada.

I’m currently in my fourth year at Simon Fraser University in the Interactive Arts and Technology major, with a minor in the Publishing Program. A lot of what I learn and do revolves around the conception of new media and design of inventive technologies. This is pretty much the “combination of the science of human experience, analysis of media and culture, the creation of original and experimental works of art, and the implementation of new technologies.” Currently, my interests and goals revolve heavily around design in general, with a focus in UX/UI design, marketing and information, web design/development, product/graphic design, and user testing and analysis.

I’m constantly learning and eager to help translate concepts into effective visual language. I'm motivated, eager to learn and always strive to make design meaningful.
