As a four-month academic project, Dad's Donair was the client for our IAT 333: Design Methods project where we explored different interaction methods and created a technology solution for the client.

THE PROCESS

content & needs

In the early stages of the process, we sought out the hierarchy of needs, a list of goals that should be met and use them as our guiding principles.

    1. Easily find diet-specific recipes, reducing hassle and discouragement
    2. Avoid ambiguity of finding suitable alternatives themselves
    3. Feel positive and encouraged to try new recipes & cuisines
